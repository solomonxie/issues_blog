# Factoring sum & division of `cubes`

[Khan lecture 1.](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra2/polynomial-functions/modal/v/factoring-sum-of-cubes)
[Khan lecture 2.](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra2/polynomial-functions/modal/v/difference-of-cubes-factoring)

```py
# Sum of cubes
a³ + b³ = (a + b)(a² - ab + b²)

# Division of cubes
a³ - b³ = (a - b)(a² + ab + b²)
```
