# `Conic Sections`

> It's something related to the previous topic `Cross sections`. It is a section (or **a slice**) through a **Cone**.

[Khan lecture.](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra2/intro-to-conics-alg2/modal/v/introduction-to-conic-sections)
[Youtube 3D animation.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HO2zAU3Eppo)
[Maths is fun.](http://www.mathsisfun.com/geometry/conic-sections.html)

By slicing through a double cone, it can create these 2D shapes:
- `circle`
- `ellipse`
- `parabola`
- `hyperbola`
