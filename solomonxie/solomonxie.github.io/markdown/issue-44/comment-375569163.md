# `Series`

## `Arithmetic Series`

> `Arithmetic Series` is the **SUM** of `Arithmetic Sequence`. Like the sum of sequence `1, 3, 5, 7`.

[Khan lecture.](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra2/sequences-and-series/modal/v/formula-for-arithmetic-series)

> `a₁` is the **FIRST** term of the sequence, and `a𝘯` is the **LAST** term. 
Simple to remember: It's the **AVERAGE** of first and last term, and multiply `n` times.
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14041622/37817263-361fefaa-2eb1-11e8-8e21-1226b78f7e5e.png)

## `Geometric Series`

> `Geometric Series` is the **SUM** of the `Geometric Sequence`. Like the sum of `1, 3, 6, 18, 54`.

[Khan lecture.](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra2/sequences-and-series/modal/v/deriving-formula-for-sum-of-finite-geometric-series)

> The trick is to multiply the `geometric sequence` with a **NEGATIVE COMMON RATIO**, and add up two sequences:
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14041622/37822099-744a89fc-2ec0-11e8-881c-d3fd95ea4067.png)


> And we will get the formula for the sum of geometric sequence:
